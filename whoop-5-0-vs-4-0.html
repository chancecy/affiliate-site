Detailed Comparison

1. Battery Life & Charging

Both models offer up to 5 days of battery life, but the WHOOP 5.0 charges much faster. A quick 15-minute charge gives you a full day of use, making it far more convenient for daily life.

2. Sensor Accuracy

The 5.0 uses an updated sensor for better heart rate, HRV, and sleep tracking. For athletes and anyone serious about recovery, this small upgrade makes real-world differences.

3. Comfort & Design

WHOOP 5.0 is lighter and slimmer, making it more comfortable to wear 24/7, especially during sleep or workouts.

Who Should Buy Which?

  • Get WHOOP 5.0 if: You’re a new buyer, want better accuracy, faster charging, or improved comfort.
  • Stick with 4.0 if: You already own it and are happy with performance.
